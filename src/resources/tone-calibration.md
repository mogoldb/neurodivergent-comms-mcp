# Tone Calibration

## Professional Levels
- Formal: External/exec
- Standard: Cross-team
- Casual: Team/1:1

## Tone Markers
- Warmth: greetings, thanks
- Directness: appropriate for urgency
- Hedging: "might", "possibly" (use sparingly)

## Red Flags
- ALL CAPS (except acronyms)
- Multiple !!!
- Sarcasm
- Unintended curtness
